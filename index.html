Biography

Full Name: Denis Ochan

Origin: Panykel Village, Pajimo Parish, Agwang Sub-county, Kitgum District, Uganda

Currently Based: Masaka, Uganda

Papa Dizzy Music is a Ugandan recording artist whose style blends Afro Dancehall, Zouk, Acholi traditional sounds, and Gospel music. He began his musical journey in 2019 when a music promoter offered him the opportunity to record his first audio.

Inspired by legendary Northern Uganda artists such as Bosmic Otim, BSG Labongo, and Labert Dickson, Papa Dizzy grew passionate about creating music that inspires and uplifts. His songs carry strong messages of life motivation, love, and the promotion of Acholi culture to the world.

One of his biggest achievements has been using his talent to return to school and support his career path, showing that music can transform lives. Papa Dizzy‚Äôs vision is to inspire communities, share African stories, and represent Acholi culture on the global stage.
